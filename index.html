Neptou

Nepal is endowed with both natural beauty and a rich cultural heritage. The tourism industry is the largest and only one that can bring in foreign revenue quickly. Unfortunately, Nepal faced a massive economic crisis and high inflation a year ago. Neptou is designed with unique features to meet the needs of tourists and maximize Nepal's tourism potential. Without the support and cooperation of the government of Nepal, Neptou could not be an official launching app. So, we went to the Nepal tourism board and had wonderful interactions with officials. We consequently received authorization and launch plans for the app.
